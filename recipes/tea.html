Aragorn's Athelas Tea

Description

Ready to get cozy? Put on your favorite bedtime smallclothes, and grab your favorite book of tall tales; you're going to want to kick your feet up with this enchanting brew. since herb-lore of Middle-Earth is mostly confined to herbs native to the Shire, records of True Athelas are scarce. However, it seems by all accounts that Mint is the closest relative.

Ingreteaents

  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 3-4 1/2 inch coins of fresh ginger
  • 1 clove of garlic ("you must trust master..")
  • 3 sprigs of mint (approximately 7-10 leaves)
  • 2L filtered water, boiling
  • Unpasteurised honey
  • Echinaccia extract, liquid

herb-lore (Steps)

  1. place ginger, mint, and garlic (peeled, not crushed) in a cheesecloth and tie off
  2. add cheesecloth bag to boiling water, and cook for 5-7 minutes
  3. turn off hear, add lemon juice
  4. remove spices and transfer to insulated tea pot
  5. serve in tea cups
  6. into each cup, add honey to taste, and echinaccia to the bottle's instructions
