Mom's Spaghetti

Description

Are your palms sweaty? Are your knees weak, or your arms heavy? Don't be nervous; you should be calm and ready.

Lose yourself in this delicious classic that will have you needing to snap back to reality. You only get once chance on this recipe...as far as I'm concerned.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 lbs ground beef
  • 1/3 lb grount Italian Sausage
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 2 14-oz cans tomato sauce
  • 1 can condenced tomato soup
  • 1/2 tsp. each:
    • Italian seasoning
    • basil
    • garlic powder
    • salt & pepper
  • 1 Tbsp. brown sugar
  • 3-4 bay leaves

Steps

  1. brown ground beef and sausage with onion
  2. drain very well; blot with paper towels if needed.
  3. add seasonings to meat and mix well
  4. add tomato sauce & soup
  5. stir in brown sugar
  6. heat to just before boiling point on medium heat, stirring often(be careful not to scorch
  7. turn heat down, add bay leaves and let simmer 30 minutes or more, stirring occasionally
  8. allow steam to escape so it's not too watery. Remove bay leaves.
  9. serve over pasta
